Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

This rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo recipe is easy enough for beginner cooks, but itâ€™s impressive enough for company.

Ingredients

  • 24 oz dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
  3. Increase the heat to mediuml stir in grated cheeses until melted and sauce has thickened.
  4. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until throughly coated; serve immediately.
